# MPV Sources & Playlists
Personal quick-access media system for mpv

Principles:
- Everything must be callable instantly
- Prefer stable streams & channels
- Playlists are mood / context based, not genre-purist
- `.m3u` is the source of truth

---

## MUSIC — BACKGROUND / FLOW

### SomaFM
Best for uninterrupted background listening.
- Groove Salad → downtempo / ambient
- Drone Zone → deep focus / reading
- Lush → melodic ambient
- DEF CON → darker electronic
- Secret Agent → retro / spy jazz vibes

https://somafm.com

---

### Radio France (high quality AAC)
Excellent sound quality, reliable streams.
- FIP → eclectic, perfect “don’t think”
- FIP World → global sounds
- FIP Rock → alternative / rock
- France Musique → classical & jazz

https://www.radiofrance.fr

---

### Jazz / Classical Radios
- WBGO (NYC jazz)
- France Musique (concerts, archives)
